Project Cheers

Hosting creative solutions to reduce loneliness and combat mental illness through artistic channels

Built with Care

At Project Cheers, our focus is on connecting young adults to an outlet to harvest new relationships, encourage to seek help, and link them to professional assistance for a better life. Below you'll see a little about who we are, what we've done, and what we're made of.

Meet the Team

Whosome Projects

Our team leaders focus on creating memorable experiences that bring you close together with like-minded individuals. Our most recent project brought together new friends through expressive painting of self-love.

Innovative Solutions

Therapy has been seen as a tabou for too long. Why suffer in silence? Connect with like-minded people with similar interests, hobbies, and struggles.

Creative Outlets

Get together for a night of paint, clay, song, dance, and countless other activities to let loose and target emotions that you've been struggling with. With professional guidance and group activities, there's nothing that you can't accomplish.
